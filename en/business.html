OUR BUSINESS

DWL Acting in Production

DWL International Foods Inc has been in the food trading industry since 2003, acting as an importer, exporter and distributor of over 30 product categories worldwide. We are globally recognized for our excellence, combining a competitive price with high quality product. Making large investments throughout the entire chain, from food production to product delivery.

We have exported to several countries worldwide including USA, China, Emirates, Latin America and the Caribbean, among others. With local offices in Brazil, USA and Portugal, we work 24/7 to better service our customers and business partners. DWL International Foods Inc 16 years delivering high quality food from the farm to the World.

DWL is specialized in selling food or any aggricultural commodities. Headquartered in Brazil, but also having offices in the US and Portugal the company has an efficient and effective global reach which makes accessible a partnership with one of the best food producers in the world: Brazil.

Northern Paraná: The Brazilian agricultural granary.

Brazil being one of the fastest world’s growing economies, exports a wide range of products to the United States and many other countries. DWL is located in a region of Brazil that is considered the “Brazilian agricultural granary”: the state of Paraná, precisely the north of Paraná.

Paraná, one of the largest producers of grains and animal protein, its soil is very fertile and is known as "terra roxa" with a topography famous for being suitable for the most varied crops.

That allows DWL produce according to the demand of its customer and we offer various packaging sizes and brands.

Data and statistics.

Today DWL collaborates to increase the presence of Brazilian products in the world. Historically, agriculture in Paraná is one of the most important bases of the economy. The main foods generated by Parana farmers with record crops are: soy, corn, coffeeand wheat.

FROM BRAZIL TO THE WORLD

The economic potential of Londrina accessible to your Company.

Due to the privileged location of DWL headquarters in Londrina-Paraná Brazil, we have contacts in different local economic sectors which will guarantee the success of the operation.

From the production to the information technology services, the highlighted sectors in Londrina are:

AGRIBUSINESS: Londrina’s origins is based around agriculture, at one point, Paraná was responsible for 60% the world’s coffee production and Londrina was its main storage.
FOOD: The region also has a variety of food and beverage companies such as pasta, candy, alcoholic drinks, organic products and many others.
MECHANICAL ENGINEERING: Companies for machining equipment parts and batteries ensures easy maintenance.
IT and Communication: About 3,000 companies makes the region a good place to innovate and automate the production process.

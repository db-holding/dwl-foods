DWL International Foods is prepared for the latest industry trends. We are specialized in negotiation, price optimization, collaborative negotiation and insertion in the international market. DWL sells its chicken products globally having an excellent relationship with its rural producers in Paraná. This region is considered the “agricultural granary of Brazil”.

Probably the product we have the most experience within our portfolio. Far East and Europe are examples of regions we have supplied chicken in the past.Full frozen or in different cuts (Breast, thigh, wings, feet) were shipped worldwide with the highest safety food requirements and international quality certificates.

    Region Highlights

  • SUSTAINABILITY: Brazil is the world’s largest producer of corn which enables us to have better prices for these commodities for the preparation of feed for our chickens which enables us to offer high quality chickens at a low price.
  • LEADER: Brazil is the world's largest chicken exporter and the world's second largest poultry producer, according to the US Department of Agriculture (USDA).
  • From Brazilian ports, almost 4 million tons are shipped annually, almost a third of all that is produced in the country.
  • COMMITMENT: With increasing export volume and working sustainably. Assessing the social and financial impact of their activities on society and preserving natural resources.

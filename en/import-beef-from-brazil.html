BRAZILIAN BEEF:

The best conditions which results in the best Beef.

For many year, our company has exported large quantities of meat to Europe and the Far East. Front and rear cuts, as well as offal have been part of our trading portfolio.

Brazil is a very important player in the meat industry worldwide, achieved through large investments in technology which has elevated not only productivity but also the quality of the Brazilian product, allowing it to become competitive reach over 150 countries worldwide.

We have access to a local network of cattle slaughters, which our potential customers are welcome to visit, we are able to deliver quality cuts at a competitive price.

DWL's headquarters is in Londrina, Brazil, south of the country which thanks to the climate, latitude, longitude and fertile land was considered the world’s agricultural capital, a privileged location to import and export. Backed by expertise of major contracts and partnerships with national and international companies.

For over 10 years, DWL has been exporting beef to: Europe, China and the Middle East. Where I already have the experience of sending plywood (30/70).

DWL's headquarters is in Londrina, Brazil, south of the country which thanks to the climate, latitude, longitude and fertile land was considered the world’s agricultural capital, a privileged location to import and export. The region's volcanic soil helps provide high quality Orange Juice. Due to its low acidity, Brazilian orange is considered excellent for the preparation of juices (concentrated or ready for consumption).

    Region Highlights

  • MADE IN BRAZIL: 60% of the World Orange Juice production is Brazilian.
  • LEADER: Due to the large production scale and low cost in the process, Brazil has become the largest producer of orange juice.
  • COMMITMENT: With increasing export volume and working sustainably. Assessing the social and financial impact of their activities on society and preserving natural resources.
